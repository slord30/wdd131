Site Plan

Site Name

Lord Family Dentistry

The business this website will be designed for is Dr. Lord who is dentist wanting to help families with their oral care.

Site Purpose

This site will help those needing quality dental care find a dentist. Many people are afraid of the dentist. This site helps ease their worries by informing them of the office, the dental services provided, how to contact the office, where the office is located, and get to know a little bit about the dental team. This helps them feel reassured and comfortable.

Scenarios

Do you see children?

Aside from the site name having family included in it, there will be pictures with families, introduction of the office's comfort dog to help, and text letting the site visitor that the office does see children.

Do you take insurance?

This dental office does take insurance, but is not in-network with any dental insurances. The site will explain the benefits of coming to a fee-for-service dental office.
